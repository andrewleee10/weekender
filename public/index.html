Kick Ball Game!!!

(Co-Ed)Come one come all

Even if you haven't played kickball since your 7th grade PE class, come on down for some Sunday Funday Fun at the Edison Park in Huntington BEach. We'll get going at 3pm and if you do decide to bring any adult beverages for the game, please make wise decisions with your vessel choices. Post game gathering to happen at sharky's where the losing kickball team will have a chance at redemption through a good old fashioned flip cup game.

location: Edison Community Park, Huntington Beach, CA

Tenaja Falls Trail Walk

Easy Trail

Let's get out and enjoy this lovely weather that we're having! Meed up at the parking lot next to the general store on the right side of the road and we'll get down to trailing! Should be enough water to take a dip so bring your swim trunks if you're feeling froggy.

location: Tenaja Trials Cleveland National Forest(parking lot next to the store)

time/date: 9:00AM Saturday, April 9th

Pick Up Soccer Game

All Skill Levels Welcome

Welcoming all skill levels for a fun pick up soccer game at Sunset Ridge Park in Newport Beach on Saturday mornings. 11 a side, no goalies, and first half of the game will be two touch to focus on our fundamentals. Be smart and safe out there and keep the contact as minimal as possible.

Sunset Ridge Park, Newport Beach, CA
